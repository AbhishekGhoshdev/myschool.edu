Admissions Process

We're excited to welcome new students to the Evergreen Academy family! Our admissions process is designed to ensure that each student is a great fit for our challenging and nurturing environment.

  1. Apply Online: Complete our comprehensive online application form.
  2. Submit Documents: Provide academic transcripts, teacher recommendations, and other required materials.
  3. Entrance Exam: Schedule and complete our standardized entrance examination.
  4. Interview: Participate in a personal interview with our admissions team.
  5. Decision: Receive our admissions decision within 4-6 weeks of completing all steps.
